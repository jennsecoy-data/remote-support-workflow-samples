# Support Ticket Quality Checklist

This checklist can be used to review email, chat, or help desk support tickets for accuracy, completeness, professionalism, and proper documentation.

## Customer Information

- Customer name or account reference is included when needed
- Contact method is documented
- Issue type is clearly identified
- Order number, ticket number, or account details are included when available
- No unnecessary private or sensitive information is included

## Issue Documentation

- Customer concern is summarized clearly
- Important details are captured accurately
- Dates, order status, tracking details, or account notes are included when relevant
- Missing information is identified
- Follow-up needs are documented

## Response Quality

- Response is professional and easy to understand
- Tone is respectful, calm, and customer-focused
- Information provided is accurate
- Resolution steps are clearly explained
- Customer questions are fully answered
- Escalation is noted when needed

## Ticket Resolution

- Action taken is documented
- Ticket status is updated correctly
- Follow-up owner is clear
- Escalation path is noted if unresolved
- Final notes are complete before closing the ticket

## Skills Demonstrated

- Customer support documentation
- Email support
- Online chat support
- Quality assurance
- Records review
- Attention to detail
- Process improvement
