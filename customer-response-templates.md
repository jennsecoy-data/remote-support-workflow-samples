# Customer Response Templates

These sample templates are fictional examples for email or live chat customer support workflows.

## Order Tracking Request

Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for reaching out. I checked the order details and confirmed that your order is currently [status]. The tracking number is [tracking number], and the latest update shows [latest tracking update].

Please let us know if you need any additional help.

## Missing Tracking Information

Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for your message. I reviewed the order and confirmed that tracking information is not available yet. I will note this for follow-up so the order status can be checked again.

We appreciate your patience and will provide an update as soon as tracking is available.

## Delayed Order

Hi [Customer Name],

I’m sorry for the delay with your order. I reviewed the information available and confirmed that the order is currently [status]. The next step is [next action].

Thank you for your patience while this is being resolved.

## Escalation Needed

Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for providing those details. I’m going to escalate this issue to the appropriate team for additional review because [reason for escalation].

I have documented the issue and included the relevant order/account details so the next team can review it efficiently.
